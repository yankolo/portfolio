This project was the final assignment in the introduction course to C# in Dawson College. The goal of the assignment was to recreate the iconic battleship game in teams of 4 in 2 weeks. The game was done using the WPF graphic subsystem. I was in charge of many of the game mechanics, including the architecture planning and the computer AI.

The project is available online at https://github.com/yankolo/battleship
